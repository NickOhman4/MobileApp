    Entourage Show/Movie

    2004-2011

    The series was created and largely written by Doug Ellin and chronicles the acting career of Vincent Chase, a young A-list movie star, and his childhood friends from Queens, New York City, as they attempt to further their nascent careers in Los Angeles.

    Harry Potter Series

    2001-2011

    Harry Potter is a series of seven fantasy novels by British author J.K. Rowling. The books follow the adventures of young wizard Harry Potter and his best friends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger. Each book corresponds to one year the trio attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

    The Princess Bride

    1987

    "The Princess Bride" is a classic fantasy romance film directed by Rob Reiner. It tells the story of a young woman named Buttercup, her true love Westley, and their journey to be reunited despite numerous obstacles. Filled with adventure, humor, and memorable characters like the swashbuckling Inigo Montoya and the villainous Prince Humperdinck, the movie combines action, comedy, and romance to create a timeless and beloved cinematic experience.

    The Bitcoin Standard

    2018

    "The Bitcoin Standard" is a book written by Saifedean Ammous that delves into the history, economics, and philosophy of Bitcoin. It explores the rise of Bitcoin as a decentralized digital currency and argues that it has the potential to become the global standard of money. The book discusses the flaws of the current fiat monetary system and highlights Bitcoin's unique properties, such as its scarcity and resistance to censorship. It also explores the implications of Bitcoin on individual freedom, economics, and the future of money.

    12 Rules for Life

    2018

    "12 Rules for Life" is a book written by Jordan B. Peterson that offers guidance and insights on how to navigate the complexities of life. It provides a set of principles and rules that aim to improve one's personal and professional life. The book combines psychology, philosophy, and personal anecdotes to address topics such as responsibility, meaning, discipline, and the importance of individual growth. It encourages readers to take control of their lives, confront challenges, and find purpose in the face of chaos and uncertainty.

    Breath

    2020

    "Breath" by James Nestor is a captivating exploration of the power and significance of breathing. Nestor delves into the science, history, and cultural practices surrounding breath, revealing its profound impact on our overall health and well-being. The book emphasizes the importance of proper breathing techniques and exposes how modern lifestyles have led to widespread breathing dysfunctions. Nestor offers practical insights and exercises to optimize our breathing patterns and improve various aspects of our lives, from physical performance to mental clarity. Overall, "Breath" serves as a compelling call to reevaluate and harness the transformative potential of our breath.
